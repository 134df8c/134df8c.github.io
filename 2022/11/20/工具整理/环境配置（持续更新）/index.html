sgx

(官方GitHub链接)[https://github.com/intel/linux-sgx]

SGX with either ME or SPS can work. Usually, ME is on desktop and embedded processors, and SPS is on server processors.


Better to contact mainboard supplier to conform SGX support, though most mainboards support SGX from my perspective. You can check BIOS options.

zsh终端

  • oh-my-zsh的插件路径：~/.oh-my-zsh/plugins

  • zsh的配置文件:~/.zshrc

在该配置文件中plugins添加自己想要的插件即可(或者在上述配置文件中添加source 对应插件的.zsh文件)，有些需要在git下载
如果要在plugins中使用对应插件的话，命名规范：插件.plugins.zsh
